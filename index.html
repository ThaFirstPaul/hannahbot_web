!hhb notification(s) add create {ping} Adds a ping to your list for hannahbot to look out for. Everyone

!hhb notification(s) allowmulti multi Toggles a users setting to allow multiple telegram accounts. Everyone
!hhb notification(s) auth authenticate, verify {auth_code} Authenticates the user for mobile notificartions. Everyone

!hhb notification(s) ignore list show (users, channels) Shows your ignored users/channels for your pings. Everyone
!hhb notification(s) ignore user u {username} Ignores a specific user in your pings. Everyone
!hhb notification(s) list mine, my, pings Returns a list of all your set pings. Everyone
!hhb notification(s) remove delete, rem, del {ping} Removes a ping from your settings. Everyone

!hhb notification(s) unignore user u {username} Un-ignores a specific user in your pings. Everyone
